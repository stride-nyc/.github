# Stride NYC

We're a technology and digital transformation consultancy. This repo is a clearinghouse for sharing our agentic skills, prompts, and AI practices.

## Skills Registry

Members share pointers to their personal repos — tools, prompts, skills, and frameworks they've built or recommend. Nothing is copied here. Ownership stays with the individual.

- Browse [`skills-registry/people/`](skills-registry/people/) for active members
- Browse [`skills-registry/alumni/`](skills-registry/alumni/) for past members

### Contributing

Submit a PR adding or modifying your file in `skills-registry/people/`. Use [`skills-registry/PERSON_TEMPLATE.md`](skills-registry/PERSON_TEMPLATE.md) as your starting point.

At least one link is required. PRs are automatically validated to confirm linked repos exist.

### Offboarding

When someone leaves Stride, their file moves from `people/` to `alumni/` via a PR.

## Social Contract

| Who | Owns |
|-----|------|
| Individual | Their personal repo, its contents, its visibility |
| Org | This index and the convention (template + validation) |

No forking, no transferring, no org access required on personal repos. People can have their own approaches before they join and keep them after they leave.
